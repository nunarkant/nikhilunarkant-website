What can you expect from this book?

AI Leadership shows how large and well-established organizations create actual value from AI and new technologies – and why that journey starts with leadership, not technology.

You will also discover:

  • Why the colour grey can help you cut through complexity
  • How rebels can turn bottom-up ideas into strategic impact
  • Why AI may become your best corporate disruption insurance
  • What penguins can teach you about AI strategy
  • What railway tracks reveal about scaling AI impact
  • What a Tuesday afternoon football practice can teach you about leading in the age of AI
They say culture eats strategy for breakfast. If that is true, then every AI strategy needs an AI culture.

That is where AI Leadership begins.
